LouisiANIME is one of Louisiana's oldest anime, gaming, arts, and Japanese pop culture festival. The 9th annual LouisiANIME will take place March 17-19, 2017 at the Baton Rouge Crowne Plaza.

Sign up for a weekend of fun filled programming and meet your favorite voice actors, Cosplayers, and more. Attendees registering to stay at the hotel now can get a special room rate of only $99 a night! Simply ask for the LouisiANIME room rate when you contact the hotel:

(225) 925-2244
4728 Constitution Ave
Baton Rouge, LA 70808

Or register online at www.crownebaton.com using LCO as the three digit reservation code! Navigate to www.crownebaton.com, click on “Accommodations” to the left, and click on “Book Now”.

Under “Make a Reservation” put in your check-in and check-out dates and click on “Check Rates and Availability”, click on "More Options" and under GROUP CODE type in your 3 digit code LCO and press BOOK.
